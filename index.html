ALPA (Anti Logic Paranormal Activity) is an experimental, non-commercial project from the Russian developer RG'ee (replenishments are possible). In fact, the story of its creation is a rather realistic dream about unrealistic things, a lot of time that has nowhere to go and the desire to create a game in the style of a visual novel or something like that. Perhaps the product itself will consist of a set of arcades, slashers, shooters, puzzles and simulators, or it may be completely canceled. That's all, and by the way, people who know about the project: 4.
